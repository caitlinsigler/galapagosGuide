Flightless Cormorant

The flightless cormorant is a truly incredible species. Unfortunately, these birds can only be observed on Fernandina island through a multiday tour. If you are interested in a multiday tour to see the cormorants, look for ones visiting Fernandina, or the Western islands, which is where their populations are most concentrated.
